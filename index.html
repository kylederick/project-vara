About Project Vara

Project Vara is dedicated to creating innovative products from captured CO2 and pioneering carbon sequestration technologies. Our mission is to transform how we think about sustainability and climate change.

Our Projects

Explore our groundbreaking projects aimed at utilizing captured CO2 and advancing carbon sequestration technologies.

Underwater Rover

Developing underwater technologies rovers for a varied amounts of usecases

Direct C02 Capture

Implementing systems to capture CO2 directly from the water as well as other environments and store it safely.

Project Phases

Phase 1: Research and Development

Focus on fundamental research, technology development, and feasibility studies.

Phase 2: Pilot Testing

Pilot-scale testing and validation of technologies to demonstrate proof of concept.

Phase 3: Commercialization

Scaling up technologies for commercial production and market entry.

Phase 4: Expansion and Innovation

Expanding production capacity, developing new applications, and driving further innovation.

Phase 5: Applications

Exploring diverse applications for captured CO2, spanning industries from materials to fuels.

Phase 6: Global Impact

Achieving a global impact on carbon emissions reduction and contributing to a sustainable future.
